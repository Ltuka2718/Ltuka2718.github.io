The vegan who travels to see family abroad? If nobody is without carbon sin, who gets to cast the first lump of coal?

The influence of movie vioence on the audience&summary

influence

In
1972, U.S. Surgeon General Jesse Steinfeld testified before
Congress that “the overwhelming consensus and the unanimous Scientific Advisory Committee’s report indicates that
televised violence, indeed, does have an adverse effect on certain members of our society”

Some violent scenes in movies may be cool for teenagers and even some adults, but there is no denying that the entertainment of violence leads to an increase in aggressive behavior daily.
Indeed, six medical and public health professional organizations held a Congressional Public
Health Summit on July 26, 2000, and issued a Joint Statement
on the Impact of Entertainment Violence on Children.

The statement also concluded that the
research points “overwhelmingly to a causal connection between media violence and aggressive behavior in some children”

What is shown here is the effect of violent video games on aggressive behavior, aggressive perceptions, aggressive emotions, helping behaviors, and physiological arousal.

summary

We’ve studied all aspects of film expression, and we’ve learned how films interact with us in different dimensions. I think we should know how to resist the bad effects of violence in movies, for example, to strengthen our anti-interference ability. On the other hand, we can also learn to use violence in movies as a model to further study people’s psychological and behavioral changes influenced by foreign objects
